Cancer Care

Cancer Care

Dr Rob has 10 years experience in oncology and is able to successfully treat most types of cancer with a combination of surgery (if required) and chemotherapy (pictured).

Chemotherapy is better tolerated in animals compared with their human counterparts.  Nevertheless it is a big decision to treat your pets cancer with chemotherapy and our vets will go through all the information to make sure you are comfortable with the process.

We are focused on extension of  life with quality. We don't believe in using chemotherapy if we can't regain your pets quality of life.
